About us

The focus the GVSU Computing Club is to learn about the various technologies in our current world while learning in a laid-back social environment. There is no expectation besides a willingness to learn, participate, and be respectful to others. The GVSU Computing Club is inclusive to everyone. If you have any interest at all stop by for our meetings: Where: Mackinac Hall, A-1-105 - EOS Computer Lab When: Thursday at 6pm Who: Anyone is welcome to participate.

Recent News

Where: Mackinac Hall, A-1-105 - EOS Computer Lab When: Thursday at 6pm Who: Anyone is welcome to participate. The GVSU Computing Club is starting up again this semester! This semester we are going to focus on various subjects within CS, such as the programming language python so we can make a bot, low level such as assembly to make a "hello world" bootloader, as well as security with an introduction to basic exploitation and/or reverse engineering. In addition, there is a goal to meet with a local company and do something special at the end of the month. So far Atomic Object, Configura and Blue Medora have responded willing to do something. If you have any questions feel free to send us an email at cclub@mail.gvsu.edu!
