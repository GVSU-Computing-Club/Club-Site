About us

The focus the GVSU Computing Club is to learn about the various technologies in our current world while learning in a laid-back social environment. There is no expectation besides a willingness to learn, participate, and be respectful to others. The GVSU Computing Club is inclusive to everyone. If you have any interest at all stop by for our meetings: Where: Mackinac Hall, A-1-105 - EOS Computer Lab When: Thursday at 6pm Who: Anyone is welcome to participate.
